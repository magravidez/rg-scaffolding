About Our Business

Since 2012, RG Scaffolding has been providing top-quality scaffolding and steelwork for sale or rent, offering durable equipment, expert support, and competitive pricing for both residential and commercial projects. The business was established by the Gravidez family in Dasmariñas, Cavite, and has expanded to other branches in Marikina, Pampanga, Tarlac, Pangasinan, and La Union.
